Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Fill Light application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in a transparent manner.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Camera Access

Our App requires access to your device's camera to provide fill light and photography functionality. We do not store, collect, or transmit any photos or videos taken through the App.

2.2 Photo Library Access

When you choose to save photos, the App requires access to your device's photo library. These photos are stored locally on your device, and we do not have access to them.

2.3 Device Permissions

The App requires permission to control screen brightness for the fill light feature. This setting only affects your device's display and is not collected or stored.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 We do not collect, store, or process any personal information.

3.2 All data and content created using the App remains solely on your device.

3.3 We do not use any tracking technologies or analytics tools.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 All content created with the App is stored locally on your device.

4.2 We do not maintain any servers or databases to store your information.

4.3 The security of your data depends on your device's security settings.

5. Third-Party Services

Our App does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services or analytics providers.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect or maintain information from persons under 13 years of age.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

7.1 We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time.

7.2 We will notify users of any material changes through the App or by other means.

8. Your Rights

8.1 You can control app permissions through your device settings.

8.2 You can delete any content created with the App through your device's storage settings.
